Nathan Jonas Jordison

The man who changed the way to play drums

Here's a time line of Joey's life:

  • 1975 - Born in Des Moines, Iowa
  • 1981 - His love for metal music begins after being at an Ozzy Osbourne concert in which Ozzy ripped a bat's head with his mouth.
  • 1986 - Starts a band called "Avaga" however for motivationless the band came to an end.
  • 1991 - He joined to Modifidious with Craig Jones, with who launch an album called "Sprawl".
  • 1995 - Paul Gray called him to invited to join Slipknot and starts his way with his most important project.
  • 1999 - Released with Slipknot their selftitle album.
  • 2001 - Released with Slipknot the Iowa Album.
  • 2004 - Replace Lars Ulrich with Metallica at Download Festival, while Lars was recovery of a stomach illness.
  • 2005 - Released with Slipknot the Vol 3: The Subliminal Verses Album.
  • 2008 - Released with Slipknot the All Hope is Gone Album.
  • 2013 - Joey leaves Slipknot after 18 years.
